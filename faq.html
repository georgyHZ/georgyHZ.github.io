Frequently Asked Questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ? In order to print a document you need to go to https://hz.mynetpay.nl, log in with your student credentials and then go to the WebPrint page. Some printing options require you to pay for the operation.
How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ? Scanning is available on every printer in the HZ building provided you have your student credentials. After scanning you should send the .pdf file to your email address.
How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop? In order for you to buy something at the HZ Web Shop, you need to go to their website - https://webshop.hz.nl, choose the shopping category that you want to browse, and if you decide to buy something, finish your payment.
How can you book a project space in one of the wings? In order to book a project space you need to go to learnHZ portal, go to "Facilities" and finally go to the Selfservice portal. Click "Make a reservation and "Reserve Meeting room or Project group room".
What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot? You can park for free in the car park at Poelendaelesingel 12 in Middelburg, the car park is a few minutes away from the HZ building, regrettably it is not currently possible to park directly on the HZ premises.
How to connect to eduoram network? In order to connect to the eduoram network you need to log into the network with your student credentials - your student email address and your password.
How to breathe? The best way to breathe is through your nose, both on the inhale and on the exhale. It is the most efficient way to breathe, as it pulls down on the lungs, creating negative pressure in the chest, resulting in air flowing into your lungs.
How to get an HZ facility card? In order to get an HZ facility card you need to set an appointment, ask for further more specific information either on reception or through Microsoft Teams.
How to apply for student finance in Netherlands? In order to apply for Student Finance (DUO) in the Netherlands you first need to get your DigID, and then you will be able to register on the Dutch government website which contains further instructions - https://www.duo.nl/particulier/.
